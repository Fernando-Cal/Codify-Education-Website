Overview

This course is an introduction to Full Stack Engineering with a particular emphasis on projects where at the end of this course you will have a wide array of projects to put on your resume when recruiting for internships and or new grad roles.

Prerequisites

While there are no formal prerequisites to take this course we do encourage you to have taken or currently taking CS61A. However it is impossible to finish this course without a formal programming background if and only if you are willing to put extra effort into the homeworks and projects.

Course Format

The course format is we meet once a week for 1.5 hours, cover the content of that weeks lecture and set aside time to build on what we just learned about. The aim of this class is to not bore
you of the content but rather excite you about the material and better prepare you for full stack engineering roles.
